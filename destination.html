Site & Destination

Andasibe - Mantadia National Park

One of the closest parks to Ivato airport and also the most visited area. This rainforest is home to a large variety of lemurs, birds, reptiles and invertebrates. It is also a good place for botanists.

Ranomafana

Rainforest in the middle southeast of Madagascar . The park is famous for the presence of the Golden Bamboo Lemurs recently discovered. It is a among the best places for wildlife.

Isalo

Geologically formed millions of years ago Isalo National Park is very attractive by its beautiful landscape of sandstone and loved by hikers. The canyons in Isalo are home to lemurs, birds and reptiles.

Zombitse

Zombitse national park crossed by the national road cannot be missed . Birdwatchers always stop there for the locally endemic birds. Zombitse if the first place to the south to see the Baobab.

Anakao Tsimanampetsotse

South of Toliara. Anakao is a fisher people s village where guests stay when they plan to go to Tsimanampetsotse National Park – two hours drive . The lake Tsimanampetsotse is a good place to spot the Flamingos . The forest is very different with a lot of medicinal plants, birds , reptiles and the locally endemic fish leaving in a cave. Anakao is also a good place to relax, for snorkelling. The visit of the sacred island Nosy ve is very suggested to see the red tailed tropic bird.
