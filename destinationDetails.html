South Itinerary

Day 1: TANA - ANTSIRABE - AMBOSITRA

Day drive to Ambositra admiring the landscape of Imerina  countrysides and terraced fields . Some stops for workshops such as aluminium cooking pot , miniatures , embroidery , and zebu horn products. 

Day 2: AMBOSITRA - RANOMAFANA

Drive to Ranomafana National Park . Get prepared for the evening walk . Afternoon relax waiting for the visit to looking for mouse lemur, one of the smallest primates  in the world , chameleons sleeping with their natural colors, frogs .

Day 3: RANOMAFANA

Day to explore the rainforest of Ranomafana National Park looking for bamboo lemurs, Sifaka,  brown lemurs , red fronted  lemurs and other types , .Strange leaf tailed  geckos sleeping with unbelievable camouflage . It is a good place for bird watchers .

Day 4: RANOMAFANA - AMBALAVAO - ISALO

Landscape changes to mountain of rocks , savannah  with termites and the view is more open . Stop in Ambalavao for workshops of Silk, Antemoro paper-paper made of bark of the tree used by descendants of Arabic people who immigrated to Madagascar. Then , visit the local park of Anja to see the Ring-tailed Lemurs. Lunch at the local restaurant. Continue the drive southwards to Isalo. Landscapes vary from savannah to sandstone rocks.

Day 5: ISALO

Full day visit of Isalo National Park. Enjoying the walk through the tapia forest and the super uniform sandstone massif. Also you can see endemic plants such as an elephant foot plants, aloe, before refreshing in the natural swimming pool . Lunch is prepared  by the local cook at the campsite where we can see the lemurs and birds.

Day 6: ISALO- TULEAR - IFATY

Drive southwards to Ifaty. Early start for an early visit at Zombitse National Park. A good place to see birds. There are also lemurs including the local endemic Zombitse Sportive Lemur and some reptiles. One of the places we can see baobabs . Lunch on the way . Then we drive through towns of saphires, and through  villages with small huts before getting to  Toliara city . Drive 20 minutes drive to arrive at Ifaty village.
Beach extension. Salary or Ambatomilo. 2 hours 30 and 4 hours from Ifaty. Anakao

West Itinerary

Day 1: TANA - MORONDAVA

Transfer to the airport for the morning flight to Morondava. After lunch, drive to Kirindy with stop at the Baobab avenue. Evening walk at the kirindy forest to see some nocturnal lemurs

Day 2: KIRINDY - BEKOPAKA

Morning visit to see more lemurs and the famous Fosa, the Madagascan largest terrestrial predator. Then drive to Bekopaka. Lunch on the way.

Day 3: MANAMBOLO CANYON, PETIT TSINGY

Boat ride on the manambolo river. Lunch at the lodge, then hike in the Petit Tsingy.

Day 4: GRAND TSINGY

After breakfast, drive to the Grand Tsingy, the main highlight of the journey. Hiking through the tsingy forest first, then inside the Karst in some caves, on top of the tsingy or in deep canyon.The view is fabulous on several points. During the visit, you can find some lemurs such as Decken’s Sifaka, Tsingy tuffed-tailed rat, Ring-tailed Mongoose, Tsingy Wood Rail, Tsingy Rock Thrush… After picnic lunch, drive back to the lodge.

Day 5: BEKOPAKA - MORONDAVA

After two days spent in Bekopaka, drive back to Morondava. Stop again at the Baobab Avenue to admire the sunset. Then drive to your hotel.

Day 6: MORONDAVA -TANA

Possible visit of the fisher people’s village before the transfer to the airport for the flight back to Tana.

Middle East Itinerary

TANA - ANDASIBE - TANA

Spending two nights in Andasibe to explore the parks to see Lemurs, reptiles, and Birds.
Day 1: Drive from Tana to Andasibe. Stops to admire the landscapes, the everyday life of the Merina tribe.
Day 2: Exploring the forests  around the area for diurnal and nocturnal visits. Rainforest to see the Indri-the biggest Lemur in Madagascar. Good site for birds as well.
Day 3: Drive back to Tana.
Interets: highland  and landascapes enroute, Lemurs, Birds , Reptiles , Insects , Plants. Drive North  to Anjozorobe  for 2 Nights 3 days.

North highland Itinerary

TANA - ANJOZOROBE - TANA

Spending two nights in Anjozorobe to explore the park to see Lemurs, Birds, and Reptiles.
Day 1: Drive from Tana to Anjozorobe. Enjoy the landscape, everyday life of the Merina tribe, working in the fields, working as black smiths, koba-Malagasy cake- making.
Day 2: Exploring the forest around the area for diurnal and nocturnal visits. Looking for Lemurs, including the biggest-the Indri.
Day 3: Drive back to Tana.
Interets: highland  and landascapes enroute, Lemurs, Birds , Reptiles , Insects , Plants

East Itinerary

TANA - PALMARIUM - ANDASIBE - TANA

Day 1: Transfer to the hotel.
Day 2: Early drive to the East coast .Stop en route for  lunch , Then boat trip to Palmarium. Enjoy the landscape en route, the everyday life of the Merina then the Betsimisaraka tribes.
Day 3: Walk around the hotel to see the lemurs .Nocturnal visit to meet the Mythic Aye aye Lemur.
Day 4: Drive back to Andasibe . Night walk visit to see nocturnal wildlife, Lemurs, geckos, frogs, and sleeping chameleons.
Day 5: Visit of Mantadia National Park  .Optional visit of the Lemur island , VOI or Mitsinjo private Parks.
Day 6: Drive back to Tana.

South - East Itinerary

TANA - TAOLAGNARO - TANA

Day 1: TANA - TAOLAGNARO - BERENTY
Early transfer to the airport for the flight to Taolagnaro . On arrival , drive to Berenty. The vegetation changes from a tropical ever green to a semi-aride types. Check in to the lodge , lunch and afternoon visit . Relax before the evening walk for the nocturnal Lemurs and Chameleons.
Day 2: BERENTY
Morning visit , a chance to see Verreaux’s Sifaka dancing and crossing the road . Good place to see birds. Lunch , then Afternoon visit of the spiny forest for more wildlife.
Day 3: BERENTY - TAOLAGNARO.
Drive back to TAOLAGNARO . Afternoon visit of Nahampoana Reserve  to see Lemurs and Chameleons
Day 4: TAOLAGNARO - TANA
Before the transfer to the airport for the flight back to Tana , city tour of the city. On arrival in Tana , transfer to the hotel

West Itinerary

TANA - MAHAJANGA - ANKARAFANTSIKA - TANA

Day 1: TANA - MAHAJANGA
Transfer to the airport for the flight to Mahajanga. Check in to the lodge . If time allows, have a little walk to the famous seaside of Mahajanga and see the big Baobab.
Day 2: MAHAJANGA - ANKARAFANTSIKA
Drive to Ankarafantsika National Park . A good place to see Lemurs and birds . Check in and lunch . Afternoon to explore tropical decidious dry forest to see Lemurs and Birds .
Day 3: ANKARAFANTSIKA
Morning  boat ride on the Ravelobe Lake to see the Madagascar Fish Eagle and other water birds. Then , take a trail to see the Baobabs . Lunch , have an afternoon drive to the canyon to see how beautiful the erosion has sculpted it.  Before dinner , take an evening walk by the Andranofasika village.
Day 4: ANKARAFANTSIKA - MAHAJANGA - TANA
Early transfer back to Mahajanga to catch the flight to Tana.

North Itinerary

TANA - ANTSIRANANA - TANA

Day 1: TANA - ANTSIRANANA
Transfer to the airport for the flight to Antsiranana . On arrival, drive to Joffreville . Check in . Relax before the evening walk to look for local nocturnal wildlife.
Day 2: AMBER MOUNTAIN NATIONAL PARK
Breakfast . Short drive to the park and have a day visit with pack lunch . Explore the forest to see Lemurs, reptiles such as leaf tailed geckos and the local endemic leaf chameleon and some birds.
Day 3: JOFFREVILLE – TSINGY ROUGE –ANDRAFIAMEN
After breakfast , drive to Andrafiamena with a stop at Tsingy Rouge- made of red marl carved by the rain drops. Lunch at the local restaurant then drive to Andrafiamena. Check in at Black Lemur camp . Evening walk to see nocturnal fauna such as Brown Mouse Lemur, Dwarf Lemur.
Day 4: ANDRAFIAMENA - ANKARANA NATIONAL PARK
Morning visit to look for the Black Sifaka. Lunch then drive to Ankarana.
Day 5: ANKARANA NATIONAL PARK
Morning visit of the dry deciduous forest to see baobabs and wildlife. Some parts of the park is formed with limestone which makes the landscape very special. Afternoon at leisure.
Day 6: ANKARANA – ANKIFY – NOSY BE
Drive to Ankify, time to admire cocoa, coffee, and ylang ylang plantation. Arriving in Ankify, take a boat to Nosy Be. Check in , lunch and afternoon at leisure.
Day 7 & Day 8: NOSY BE
The activities you can do during your stay :

  • Visit of Nosy Komba island for lemurs and reptiles.
  • Visit of Nosy Tanikely island or Nosy sakatia for some snorkelling
  • Visit of Nosy Iranja island for beach
