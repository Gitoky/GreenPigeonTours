Andasibe-Mantadia National Park

One of the closest parks to Ivato airport and also the most visited area. This rainforest is home to a large variety of lemurs, birds, reptiles and invertebrates. It is also a good place for botanists.

Ranomafana

Rainforest in the middle southeast of Madagascar . The park is famous for the presence of the Golden Bamboo Lemurs recently discovered. It is a among the best places for wildlife.

World fourth largest Island, Madagascar is actually a mini-continent with several climate types, a wide array of geomorphological specialties and multiple vegetation types: a journey in Madagascar for a different scenery is always rewarding. Madagascar was part of the ancient cinnamon route between South-east Asia and Egypt. Sailors from the indo-Malaysian area did regular visits on the Malagasy Coasts. As far in time as 15 centuries BC, people from different origins did visit Madagascar with theses sailors and even settled in: indo-Malaysian, Arabic, bantou…As a result, there are many different custom types in the Island and if one is fond of other people’s culture, a journey in Madagascar is more than interesting and Green Pigeon Tours will organize it nicely. Talking about the flora and fauna of Madagascar is talking of superlatives: the most baobabs in the world, more orchids than the whole Africa, 19 of the strange elephant foot are Malagasy and only 5 in Africa, three times more palm tree species than the whole Africa, world largest and world smallest chameleon, birds with one of the world highest level of endemism, one of the world richest country in primates…A journey in Madagascar for its biodiversity will always be worth it.
